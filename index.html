About Birdies in the Burg

Welcome to Birdies in the Burg – your ultimate guide to disc golfing in Harrisonburg, Virginia! Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting out, our guide brings together the best courses, insider tips, and community resources to help you make the most of the thriving disc golf scene in the heart of the Shenandoah Valley.

Our mission is to connect players with the beauty of Harrisonburg’s parks and the excitement of disc golf, from hidden trails to popular local courses. Discover course reviews, maps, and tips to improve your game, all created by locals who love the sport just as much as you do.

Join us on the course, find your next birdie, and explore what makes disc golfing in “the Burg” unique. Let’s play!

Disc Golf Courses in Harrisonburg

Westover Park

Nestled in the heart of Harrisonburg, Westover Park offers an 18-hole course with a mix of open fields and wooded areas. This course is great for players of all skill levels, with scenic views and challenging layouts.

Hillandale Park

Located in a beautiful, wooded area, Hillandale Park's 21-hole disc golf course provides a peaceful setting with various elevation changes. It’s a favorite among locals for its technical shots and well-maintained trails.

Heritage Oaks

Heritage Oaks offers a 9-hole course that’s perfect for beginners or those looking for a quick game. Set against the backdrop of rolling hills, this course combines open spaces with a few challenging obstacles.
